# SpillStack Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 29, 2024**

## Introduction

SpillStack ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Voice Recordings**: When you use the voice capture feature, your audio is temporarily processed to convert speech to text. Voice recordings are sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription.
- **Text Content**: Ideas, notes, and tasks you create within the app.
- **URLs**: Links you save for later reference.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app to improve our services.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the SpillStack service
- Process voice recordings into text using AI transcription
- Organize and categorize your ideas using AI assistance
- Improve and optimize the app experience
- Send you updates about the app (with your consent)

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your ideas, tasks, and notes are stored locally on your device using secure storage.
- **Cloud Processing**: Voice recordings are temporarily sent to OpenAI for transcription. We do not store your voice recordings on our servers.
- **No Account Required**: SpillStack works without requiring you to create an account.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### OpenAI
- **Purpose**: Voice transcription (Whisper API) and AI-powered text enhancement (GPT-4)
- **Data Shared**: Voice recordings (for transcription), text content (for AI processing)
- **Privacy Policy**: [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)

### Uploadcare (if applicable)
- **Purpose**: File storage for voice recordings
- **Data Shared**: Audio files
- **Privacy Policy**: [Uploadcare Privacy Policy](https://uploadcare.com/about/privacy-policy/)

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- **Voice Recordings**: Processed in real-time and not permanently stored by us.
- **AI Processing**: Text sent to OpenAI is subject to their data retention policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored locally on your device within the app.
- **Delete**: Remove any ideas, tasks, or notes at any time.
- **Export**: Export your data from the app.
- **Opt-out**: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

SpillStack is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Local data is stored using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS
- All network communications use HTTPS encryption
- Voice recordings are transmitted securely to third-party services

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

**Email**: privacy@spillstack.app

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights regarding their personal information:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## International Users

If you are accessing SpillStack from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

---

By using SpillStack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this privacy policy.
